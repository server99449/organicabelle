Meet the Founder: Ruchi Sinha

Ruchi Sinha, the visionary behind Orgenicabelle, started this venture with a mission to provide high-quality, chemical-free skincare soaps for better skin health. Her dedication to purity and excellence has helped Orgenicabelle grow to over 3,000 customers, with Aloe Vera Charcoal Soap becoming the most in-demand product. With a strong vision to establish Orgenicabelle as a leading skincare brand, Ruchi is also committed to empowering women by creating job opportunities in soap-making factories, fostering both economic growth and sustainable skincare solutions.
